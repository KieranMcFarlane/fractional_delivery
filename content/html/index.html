THE CHALLENGES

When teams usually call me

When growth starts to feel like chaos, it’s usually because the delivery systems haven't scaled with the headcount.

  • Stalling Velocity

    Delivery keeps slowing down as the team grows and complexity increases.

  • Slipping Roadmaps

    Deadlines are consistently missed and roadmaps slip every quarter.

  • Outcome Friction

    Engineers feel busy and productive, but the actual business outcomes don’t move.

  • Priority Overload

    Priorities change faster than work completes, leading to wasted effort.

  • Fragile Scale

    Leadership wants scale, but the current delivery foundation feels too fragile to hold it.
