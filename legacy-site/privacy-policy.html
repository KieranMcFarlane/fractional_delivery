Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Fractional Delivery (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard personal data when you visit fractionaldelivery.com.

This Privacy Policy is provided in accordance with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Fractional Delivery is a digital delivery consulting service.

If you have any questions about this policy or your personal data, you may contact:

  • Email: camille@fractionaldelivery.com
  • Website: fractionaldelivery.com

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal data:

Information you provide directly

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company name
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Any information submitted through contact forms, email signups, or meeting bookings

Technical data automatically collected

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of visits
  • Referring website

This information may be collected using analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) or standard server logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • Responding to enquiries submitted through the website or Cal.com
  • Providing information about our consulting services
  • Sending email updates to signed-up subscribers
  • Improving website performance and user experience using Analytics insights
  • Maintaining website security

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interest – to respond to enquiries and operate the website
  • Consent – where users voluntarily submit personal data through forms, bookings, or email subscriptions

5. Data Sharing

We may share personal data with trusted service providers that support our website operations and business functions, such as:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Booking and scheduling tools (e.g., Cal.com)
  • Email service and newsletter providers

These providers only process data on our behalf and in accordance with data protection laws.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, including responding to enquiries, providing services, and maintaining business records.

7. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us at camille@fractionaldelivery.com.

8. Cookies

This website uses cookies and similar technologies (managed by Google Analytics) to improve functionality and analyse website usage.

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
